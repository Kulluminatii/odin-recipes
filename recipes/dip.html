Skillet Spinach Dip

Description

Homemade spinach dip make for a fantastic appetizer. Make sure to use an oven proof skillet, such as a cast iron, to bake the rolls and the dip at the same time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 4 Tbsp butter, unsalted
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Butter cast iron skillet
  3. Make spinach dip
